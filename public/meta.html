Strategy Tips

  • Play around map control: Don't just rush for kills. Establish position.
  • Avoid easy counters: Don’t first-pick brawlers that are easily countered.
  • Tank usage: Use tanks only when the map has enough walls to support them.
  • Solo Queue survival: Pick brawlers that can survive mistakes when playing with randoms.

What is a Player Tag?

Your Player Tag is a unique identifier assigned to your Brawl Stars account. It typically starts with a hashtag (#) followed by a combination of letters and numbers (e.g., #2CQJLLUGUV). This tag is used to publicly track your progress, trophies, and brawler statistics without needing your username or password.

You can find your tag by opening the Brawl Stars app, tapping on your profile icon in the top left corner, and looking right below your profile picture.

How Player Lookup Works

Brawl Stars Tracker uses the official Supercell API to securely fetch public data about your account. When you enter your player tag, our system retrieves real-time statistics including your total trophies, 3v3 victories, solo/duo wins, and individual brawler performance.

This tool is designed to help you analyze your gameplay, track your rank progression, and see which brawlers you perform best with.

How Meta Rankings Work

Our Meta Guide provides a community-driven look at the current state of the game. We analyze pick rates, win rates, and recent balance changes to determine which brawlers are currently dominating the arena.

Whether you're looking for the best brawler for Solo Showdown, or a synergistic team pick for Brawl Ball, our tier lists and strategy guides are updated regularly to reflect the latest meta shifts.

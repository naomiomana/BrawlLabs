About Brawl Stars Tracker

Our Mission

Brawl Stars Tracker was created by passionate players who wanted a cleaner, faster, and more insightful way to track their in-game progress. Our mission is to provide the community with an accessible dashboard to view player statistics and a comprehensive guide to understanding the current meta.

Features

Our Player Lookup tool uses the official Brawl Stars API to pull real-time data about your trophies, victories, and brawler collections. Our Meta Guide curates the best picks, strategies, and balance changes to keep you ahead of the competition.
